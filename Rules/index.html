Rules

Self explanatory. Our ingame chats are linked to our discord server.

Hacking is not allowed on our network unless otherwise stated in a specific subserver. Optimization mods and shaders are generally okay, but X-ray, cheats and exploits are not allowed.

This includes alt accounts for boosting, book bans, etc.

Purposly exploiting glitches (without reporting) will result in an immediate ban. You have been warned.

Don't spam the Discord or Minecraft chats.

Content which is of a not safe for work context is forbidden on this server.

Any hate towards any group is not tolerated on our Minecraft server.

Except in dedicated channels, all advertising is forbidden. Screenshots and videos are okay, but directly linking YouTube channels is not.

This server is generally an english speaking server. While we wish to accept other languages, we simply do not have the resources to moderate for other languages. For the time being, other languages are prohibited.

Any threat in an IRL context (doxxing, death threats, etc) is forbidden. Report any rulebreakers in our ticket system, and a moderator will have a look.

Generally speaking, moderators have the final say. Please make a ticket if you believe a moderator judged unfairly, and an admin or I will personally investigate.
